About Gliyarmo

About us


Welcome to our educational platform, a student-focused website designed to make learning easier, smarter, and more accessible. Our goal is to support students at different academic levels by providing reliable study resources, interactive tools, and clear guidance in one place.


This website is built especially for school and board-level students who want to prepare better for exams, understand difficult topics, and improve their overall academic performance. We focus on simplicity, accuracy, and usefulness so that students can learn without confusion.


What We Offer


  • Board Questions: Carefully organized board and exam-style questions for different classes and subjects, written in a clean and easy-to-read format.
  • Weekly Educational Blogs: Regular blog posts covering study tips, exam preparation strategies, subject explanations, and academic guidance.
  • Question Generator: A smart tool that helps students generate practice questions for revision and self-assessment.
  • Question Solver: An educational tool that provides step-by-step solutions and explanations to help students understand concepts, not just answers.
  • Translator: A simple translation tool to help students understand content across languages.
  • Student Helpdesk: A support section where students can find answers to common questions and get guidance related to studies and website usage.

Our mission is to create a safe, legal, and student-friendly learning environment that encourages curiosity and independent learning.
