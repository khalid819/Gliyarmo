Gliyarmo Documentation


1. Purpose of the Website

The purpose of this website is to provide educational assistance to students through study materials, interactive learning tools, and helpful academic content. The platform is intended for learning and practice only.


2. Website Structure

The website is structured to ensure easy navigation and accessibility:

  • Home– Overview of the website and featured content
  • Board Questions– Class-wise and subject-wise questions
  • Blog– Weekly educational articles and study guides
  • Tools
    • Question Generator
    • Question Solver
    • Translator
  • Student Helpdesk– FAQs and academic support
  • Contact– Communication and feedback

3. Content Policy

  • All educational content is written or curated carefully to avoid copyright violations.
  • Board questions are presented in a reformatted and explanatory manner for educational use.
  • No copied PDFs, pirated books, or illegal materials are published.
  • Content is suitable for students and free from adult, violent, or misleading material.

4. Tools Usage Guidelines


Question Generator
  • Used for creating practice questions
  • Generated content is for learning and revision purposes only

Translator
  • Helps users understand content in different languages
  • Translation accuracy may vary depending on text complexity

5. Disclaimer

The information provided on this website is for educational purposes only. While we strive for accuracy, we do not guarantee that all information is free from errors. Students are advised to verify important academic information with teachers or official sources.


6. Privacy & Safety

  • We respect user privacy and do not collect unnecessary personal data.
  • Any collected data is used only to improve website functionality and user experience.
  • The website is safe for students and complies with standard content policies.
  • We do not share user data with third parties without consent.
  • Our platform is safe for students and follows standard privacy, security, and content policies.

7. Future Improvements

We continuously work to improve the platform by adding:

  • More practice questions
  • Better explanations and examples
  • Improved tools and features
  • Enhanced student support options

8. Contact & Feedback

We value student feedback. If you have suggestions, corrections, or questions, please contact us through the Contact page. Your feedback helps us improve and serve students better.


Thank you for using our educational platform. Learn, practice, and grow with confidence.
